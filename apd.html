Welcome to the Academic and Professional Development Department

The Academic and Professional Development Department aims to provide learning activities for surgical care professionals, career aspirants, and the community to achieve better surgical services.

Strategic Areas

Laproscopic Surgery

Focuses on training healthcare professionals in laparoscopic techniques, improving surgical skills and patient outcomes by promoting minimally invasive surgery practices

Clinical Audits

Aims to train health professionals on Clinical Auditsto enhances the quality of surgical practices through systematic reviews and audits of clinical procedures, identifying areas for improvement and ensuring compliance with safety and care standards

SCSD Fellowship

The SCSD fellowship programme aims to develop high-quality surgical leaders with advanced knowledge, skills, research and management capabilities to contribute to the surgical services improvement in Sudan.

Surgical Competency

Focuses on developing and assessing the surgical skills of healthcare professionals, ensuring they meet high standards of proficiency in performing surgeries

Projects

Clinical Audits Training

Provides specialized training in conducting clinical audits, helping healthcare professionals assess and improve the quality of surgical care and patient safety

Research Methodology Course

Offers a comprehensive course in research methodology, teaching healthcare professionals how to design, conduct, and analyze medical research effectively.

Laparoscopic Surgery Training Program

A structured training program aimed at teaching laparoscopic surgery techniques, focusing on enhancing surgical competency and safety in minimally invasive procedures.

Exams Preperatory Courses

Prepares healthcare professionals for board exams (e.g. MRCS) offering focused training on the required knowledge and skills for certification.

MedSIN-Sudan Surgical Savvy Manual

Develops a comprehensive manual that provides expert guidance on surgical practices in Sudan, helping healthcare professionals enhance their surgical skills and improve patient outcomes.

Surgical Anatomy Series

Provides educational content on human surgical anatomy, aiming to improve the understanding of anatomical structures and enhance surgical precision

Fellowship program

The SCSD fellowship programme aims to develop high-quality surgical leaders with advanced knowledge, skills, research and management capabilities to contribute to the surgical services improvement in Sudan
