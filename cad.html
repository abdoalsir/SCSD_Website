Welcome to the Communication and Advocacy Department

The Communication and Advocacy Department aims To advocate for an efficient Sudan surgical system by establishing and maintaining effective stakeholders’ communication and technically supporting the SCSD’s departments in achieving their aim.

Strategic Areas

SCSD's Visual Identity(Brand)

We prioritize effective communication, public relations, and branding to engage stakeholders, promote SCSD’s vision, and strengthen partnerships. These efforts boost visibility, build a positive image, and amplify the impact of our work in enhancing Sudan’s surgical system.

Sudan - NSOAP

Our NSOAP advocacy project promotes the adoption and implementation of the National Surgical, Obstetric, and Anesthesia Plan in Sudan. By working with policymakers, healthcare providers, and stakeholders, we aim to decentralize surgical services, enhance training, and improve infrastructure for equitable access to quality surgical care.

Community Engagement and Mobilisation

We organize awareness campaigns to educate the public about national surgical issues, promote preventive measures, and encourage proactive healthcare behaviors. These efforts aim to create an informed and engaged community that supports improvements in Sudan's surgical system.

Projects

NSOAP Advocacy Project

Our NSOAP advocacy project promotes the adoption and implementation of the National Surgical, Obstetric, and Anesthesia Plan in Sudan. By working with policymakers, healthcare providers, and stakeholders, we aim to decentralize surgical services, enhance training, and improve infrastructure for equitable access to quality surgical care

Prostate Cancer Awareness Campaign

Raises awareness about prostate cancer, its prevention, and early detection, aiming to reduce the disease's impact through education and community outreach.

Breast Cancer Awareness Campaign

Focuses on increasing awareness about breast cancer, promoting early screening, and educating the public on prevention and treatment options.

Burn Awareness Campaign

Educates the community on burn prevention, first aid, and the importance of proper care to reduce injuries and improve recovery outcomes.

Diabetic Foot Awareness Campaign

Promotes healthy eating habits for diabetes prevention and management, educating the public on balanced diets and the role of nutrition in managing diabetes.
