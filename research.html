Welcome to the Research Department

The Research Department aims to build a scientific research and surgical quality improvement environment that reflects Sudan’s specific surgical issues and further informs the surgical policies and systems development

Strategic Areas

Research Capacity Building

Focuses on developing the skills and knowledge of healthcare professionals in research methodologies, data analysis, and scientific writing to enhance the quality and impact of medical research.

Research Databases

Creates and maintains comprehensive research databases, ensuring easy access to relevant medical data and research findings, thus supporting evidence-based practices and informed decision-making in healthcare.

SCSD Handbook in Research and Clinical Audit

Focuses on developing surgical-oriented handbooks that provide detailed guidelines for clinical audits, research methodologies, and best practices to improve surgical outcomes and maintain high research standards.

Health System Rubilding

Aims to strengthen and rebuild healthcare systems, particularly in conflict-affected regions, by conducting research that addresses critical gaps and improving healthcare delivery infrastructure.

Journal Clubs

Provides a collaborative space where members discuss and critically evaluate both published papers and their own research, fostering knowledge sharing and improving research quality in the medical field.

Projects

Research Training

Focuses on enhancing the research skills of healthcare professionals, providing training in research methodologies, data analysis, and scientific writing to improve the quality of medical research.

SCSD's Handbooks

Develops specialized handbooks focusing on surgical research and clinical audits, providing practical guidelines for enhancing surgical practices, improving clinical outcomes, and ensuring high standards in medical research and audits.

Conflict Research Projects

Investigates the impact of the ongoing conflict in Sudan on health systems, focusing on the challenges and needs in healthcare delivery, with a goal to improve medical responses and aid.

Journal Clubs

Provides a platform for discussing medical research papers, where members review and critique both published and internal research to enhance scientific knowledge and foster collaboration.
